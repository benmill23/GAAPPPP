The Concept

More Than Trivia Night

World Historic Fare Flights isn't your typical pub quiz. It's a carefully crafted experience where college-level history presentations meet artisanal cuisine, local craft beer, and genuine community impact.

Every event at New Heights Brewery benefits Fort Negley, Nashville's historic Civil War site, through a partnership with Nashville Parks Foundation.

"Funlanthropy"—fun + philanthropy. Learn something fascinating, enjoy incredible food and beer, and help preserve Nashville history. All at once.

Teams of 2-6 compete through an engaging 75-minute presentation with embedded trivia questions. No phones or computers allowed—just good old-fashioned knowledge, teamwork, and a dash of luck in the final "Cornucopia Whole" game.

Game Night Flow

How It Works

From arrival to prizes, here's what your evening looks like.

1

Arrive & Settle In

Find your team's table with your game card already waiting. Tell the staff your beer preferences, and they'll customize your flight of six. Your tray of themed small bites arrives shortly after.

2

Learn & Answer

Professor William Harwood leads a fascinating 75-minute presentation. Six multiple-choice questions are embedded throughout—mark your answers on your team's game card. No phones or computers allowed!

3

Tally the Scores

Turn in your game cards to the judges' table. The number of correct answers determines how many bean bags your team earns for the final game (plus one "participation" bag just for showing up).

4

Play Cornucopia Whole

Each team tosses their bean bags at a cornhole board with a cornucopia in the hole. Score points: 5 for the cornucopia, 3 for staying on the board, 2 for touching, 1 for floor. Highest total wins the Grand Prize!

5

Take Home Your Prizes

Everyone leaves with a GAAPPPP Prize Packet—swag, coupons, leftover bites, and beer samples. The winning team gets the Grand Packet with the best goodies. Hungry? The food trucks outside are ready for dinner!
